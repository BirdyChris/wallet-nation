Stablecoin Merchant Payments

Drop into existing stablecoin payment rails — without disruption.

Wallet Nation adds non-custodial payment protection directly into existing stablecoin merchant rails — with no wallet migration, no new custody arrangements, and no changes to your core stack.

The risk
Zero migration

Merchants adopting stablecoin payments often face a choice between speed and protection. Wallet Nation removes that trade-off by introducing conditional settlement without requiring new wallets or custodial accounts.

Rail compatibility

Key benefits

No wallet migration

Existing merchant wallets stay exactly as they are. Wallet Nation adds protection at the settlement layer, not the wallet layer.

No new custody

Funds are never held by Wallet Nation. Protection is enforced through cryptographic control, not a custodial account.

No stack changes

Integrate alongside current payment infrastructure — no re-platforming, no new rails, no customer re-education.
